An explanation of the rules can be found at www.hanafubuki.org. To play a card drag it onto the card you wish to capture or an empty space (valid targets will be highlighted). Current house rules:

  • If a player decides to koikoi the multiplier for both players doubles. This is the only way it changes.
  • Score is not doubled if you have over 7 points.
  • Flower viewing and Moon viewing are both active.
  • The sake cup only scores as junk (dregs), not animal.
  • Special draws don't score. They're silently re-drawn in the background.
Rule variants may be made available later.

The client has been tested in Firefox, Opera and Chromium (Chrome). It should work in any modern browser with proper javascript support.
